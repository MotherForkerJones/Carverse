# Carverse
The ledger. Your car. Your city. Your tower. Your scene.

CARVERSE  
Take Over The Streets

This is the app that turns real car culture into something you live every single day.

Open it and you’re in the feed — raw posts of builds, night drives, engine work, meets, and crew talk. Scroll, like, comment, shoot offers, slide into DMs. Local tab shows exactly what’s happening in your city right now.

Profiles show your cars, your current rank on The Ledger (the top-15 that actually matters), and your Street Cred from races and deals.

Swipe screen: nearby cars and drivers pop up. Swipe right to challenge. They swipe back — race is locked in. You pick the terms: casual or with a wager. Smart contract holds everything. Race happens at a legal track or organized event only. After the run, the contract pays the winner instantly — minus the 1% fee that goes straight to the community treasury.

Marketplace is built right in. List your car, parts, or full build. Attach the UDI (its on-chain history of mods, runs, and notes) so buyers know exactly what they’re getting. Escrow keeps it safe. Every completed deal, wager, or service pays the same 1% fee into the treasury.

The Ledger is straight competition. Top 15 only. Challenge anyone above you. Win and you take their spot. Defend it or get knocked down. All wins, losses, and rivalries recorded on-chain forever.

The Garage Tower is the spot.

This is the multi-floor takeover HQ the crew actually lives in.

- Ground floor: Pull up any day. Service shops for quick fixes, vendor stalls with parts and wraps, hangout areas where you park, pop the hood, talk shit, and vibe with the same faces.
- Mid-levels: Real workshops for serious builds, space for RC racing to test setups, and rooms for smaller crew events.
- Rooftop: Every weekend it turns into the party — DJ sets, rooftop raves, cars lined up, bass hitting, lights going, city skyline in the background. People posted up from sunset till late.

Every floor gives you a reason to stay: fix your shit on the ground, build on the mid-levels, rage on the roof.

The entire tower is funded by the 1% fee from marketplace trades, wagers (including Ledger race payouts), and services. No sponsors. No corporate bullshit. Run by the players, built for the players.

Everything is 100% voluntary.  
You agree to the Terms of Service when you enter. No one can touch your funds. All racing is legal — track events or organized venues only. The app just matches the race, holds the wager, and pays out clean. The Ledger only records official results.

Check in at the tower through the app and see who’s already there. Post from the roof and it hits the feed. Daily hangs turn into weekend takeovers because the tower is ours — funded by the streets, run by the crew.

Non-custodial. Your keys, your cars, your money.
